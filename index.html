biography

Rapha has been creating with code and multimedia tools since 2002, having studied algorithms and formal systems.

His work has been exhibited at the Centre Pompidou (Paris), the NFT Museum (Seattle), and Beeple's Studio (NC).

He's also a passionate collector of generative and AI art, and, for good measure, an inline skater.
Rapha has been exploring text-to-image AI since 2022, focusing mostly on abstract outputs, and often using code-generated images as a starting point.
Penumbra explores the idea of a slowly evolving render: each output is produced by a directional "shadow" which revolves around a central point over a 1 year period, producing a total of 365 images.

Curated by Koda.art
